THE STORY

Born in Indore, Madhya Pradesh, I've always been the person who asks "why" when everyone else accepts "what." This curiosity led me from being the youngest Indian speaker at the United Nations Youth Assembly to building businesses and eventually creating conversations that matter.

My journey isn't just about entrepreneurship or podcasting—it's about understanding human potential. Every conversation, every question, every moment of curiosity has been building toward one goal: helping people figure out the complexities of modern life.

From addressing world leaders to sitting across from Vijay Mallya for 4.3 hours, from writing bestselling books to introducing concepts like "Emotional ROI," the thread that connects everything is simple: the right questions change everything.

"Some people meditate for peace. I ask questions."

Today, through "Figuring Out with Raj Shamani," I get to explore the minds behind success, failure, innovation, and human resilience. Because at the end of the day, we're all just figuring it out— one conversation at a time.
