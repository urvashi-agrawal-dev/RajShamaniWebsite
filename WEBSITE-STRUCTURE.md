## 🎨 Design Characteristics

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: Muted Gold (#D4AF37)
- **Background**: Pure Black (#000000)
- **Text**: White (#FFFFFF) and Gray (#CCCCCC)
- **Accents**: Gold gradients and glows

### Typography
- **Headings**: Poppins (800 weight)
- **Body**: Inter (300-700 weights)
- **Letter spacing**: Consistent, premium feel

### Animations
- **Hero**: Slow zoom parallax (30s)
- **Scroll**: Fade-in, slide-up on scroll
- **Hover**: Scale, glow, color transitions
- **Counters**: Animated count-up
- **Logo**: Glow pulse (4s infinite)
- **Timing**: 0.3-0.9s durations, power3.out easing

### Layout
- **Max width**: 1400px
- **Section padding**: 5rem vertical
- **Grid gaps**: 3-6rem
- **Responsive**: Mobile-first approach
