THE STORY

Born in Indore, Madhya Pradesh, I've always been driven by an insatiable curiosity about what makes extraordinary people extraordinary. This curiosity led me to become the youngest Indian speaker at the United Nations Youth Assembly.

From building businesses to asking the questions that matter, my journey has been about understanding human potential and sharing those insights with the world.
